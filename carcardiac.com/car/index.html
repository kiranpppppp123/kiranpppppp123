About Us

Vehicles that run using fuel does so by burning the fuel inside combustion chamber. When anything burns, smoke and soot is formed. Only 80% of the carbon so formed during combustion is ejected out through the exhaust. Rest 10% is removed through lubrication oil change. Remaining about 10% remains inside the combustion chamber on the valves, cylinder head, piston-top, cylinder wall etc.
This sticky carbon is the silent killer of vehicle’s engine. Because this carbon build up reduces the air suction capacity, thus affecting the combustion efficiency. This results in reduced throttle response, reduced power, mileage and smoothness, also increases noise, vibration, emission & black smoke.

But do not worry, We have introduced the POH  ( Pure Oxygen & Hydrogen) carbon cleaning technology for the first time in India. Through this we can remove carbon in just 30 minutes without dismantling the engine.

Frequenty Asked Questions

Doesn't hydrogen and oxygen just make water in my engine?

No. The hydrogen does not reconstitute back into water.

What actually oxygen and hydrogen does inside engine?

Hydrogen and oxygen gas enter the engine through the air intake in diesel vehicles or through vacuum pipe in petrol engines. The Hydrogen and oxygen gas then reacts with the unburnt or semi-burnt fuel to remove it as CO2 and water vapor molecules.

Does this service involve any risks?

This is a service to remove carbon build-up. If your vehicle has a pre-existing issue or fault which is being masked by the carbon build up and perhaps the carbon is the only thing still holding a part together, (e.g. a failing catalytic converter) then the removal of the carbon will expose the issue.
